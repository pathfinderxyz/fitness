The message is , in the most general sense , the object of communication. It is defined as information that the sender sends to the receiver via a communication channel or through specific communication (such as speech or writing, for example). However , the term is also applied , depending on the context , the presentation of such information; ie the symbols used to convey the message. Whatever the case, the message is a critical part in the process of information exchange .
